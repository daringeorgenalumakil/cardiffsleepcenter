About Dr Jose Thomas

Consultant in Respiratory & Sleep Medicine

Jose Thomas’ clinical experience spans over three decades and he has been a Consultant Respiratory Physician since 1995. He specialised in Sleep Medicine and sleep technology and led the development of the Sleep Medicine Centre for the Aneurin Bevan University Health Board. He leads the Aneurin Bevan Sleep Centre. He is also trained and experienced in Cognitive Behaviour Therapy (CBT) and has expertise in the treatment of Insomnia, Circadian Rhythm Sleep Disorders and other sleep problems with a behavioural component.

How can we help you?

Sleep Disorders

Accurate diagnosis is essential for timely and effective treatment. As an Expert Somnologist, Dr Thomas has wide experience in the diagnosis and management of the full spectrum of sleep disorders and can help you arrive at an early diagnosis and advice the most effective treatment.

Obstructive Sleep Apnoea

If used correctly, CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure) therapy is 100% effective in the treatment of significant Obstructive Sleep Apnoea (OSA). We can help you get started on CPAP therapy quickly. If you experience difficulties in tolerating CPAP therapy we can helpyou get used to CPAP or explore alternative options for treatment of sleep apnoea.

Sleep Studies

We carry out home sleep apnoea tests and actigraphy. We arrange full Polysomnography and other advanced sleep studies at Wales’ leading Centre for Sleep Medicine, the Aneurin Bevan Sleep Centre.

Snoring

Snoring and mild sleep apnoea are probably best treated by measures other than CPAP therapy. We can help you choose the treatment most suitable for you.

CBT for Insomnia

The most effective treatment for Chronic Insomnia Disorder is Cognitive Behaviour Therapyfor Insomnia (CBT-I). At the Cardiff Sleep Centre we are trained and experienced in both sleep medicine and sleep psychology, and have specificexpertise in CBT for Insomnia.

Unexplained breathlessness

The cause of breathlessness may remain elusive after assessment by primary care doctors, and specialists in Respiratory Medicine and Cardiology. Dr Thomas has a special interest in the evaluation of difficult cases of breathlessness.
